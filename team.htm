The Crew

Ritesh Agrawaal,founder

Ritesh started his business career at 17, backpacking around the country, living in budget accommodations. This sparked the idea of OYO, a global-first business developed in India. Driven by a fevered energy only start-ups can have, Ritesh scaled OYO from 5 cities in December 2014 to 200 cities in 2015. Ritesh was the first resident Indian to be awarded the Peter Thiel Fellowship.
